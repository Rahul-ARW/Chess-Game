Two Player Chess

A complete two-player chess game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Game Info

Current Turn: White
Status: Game started. White to move.
Selected Piece: None

How to Play

  • Click a piece to select it.
  • Click a highlighted square to move.
  • Green square = selected piece.
  • Blue dots = legal moves.
  • Red square = king in check.
